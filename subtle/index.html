The moves, one line each

  • Acquisition: fewer popup steps, skip buttons everywhere, offer visible with a dynamic code and expiry.
  • Recovery: split cart, checkout and browse into prospect vs customer versions; the first email carries the offer.
  • Activation: deepen Welcome and Post Purchase beyond 2 and 4 emails; ₹3,580 is a considered purchase and the second order decision happens inside 31 days.
  • Replenishment and cross-sell: Cloud Tee buyers get the Flow Tee story and colour restocks in the first month.
  • Win-back: later, once the list and flows above are earning.
