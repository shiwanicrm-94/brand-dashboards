Sort the welcome trigger first

The flow fires on "Customer Created" and 591 people entered in 18 days, while the popup collected 415 phone numbers. Before trusting any WhatsApp number, pin down what Kwik Engage counts as a created customer. Names lie; definitions don't.

The gaps, one line each

  • Offer in every message. It is invisible today.
  • Browse abandonment: missing. Build it with per-product messages; Kwikpass recognises the window shoppers we are currently losing.
  • Abandoned checkout: exists. Abandoned cart: missing, add it.
  • Post purchase transactional all present (confirmation, fulfilled, delivered). Cross-sell and upsell after them: missing, and easy with two categories.
  • Campaigns: keep pushing Kwikpass-recognised visitors that their welcome offer is waiting.

Restructure the popup

Email and phone appear late and every step is compulsory, with too many questions for a list this size. Move email and phone to stages 2 and 3, cut the question count, add skips. This alone should take signups from 11% to about 25% as a starter; A/B if needed, but this is the preferred build. The 27-30% target is the step-2 audience we already have.

  • The gap is step 2 to submit: 3,777 engage the quiz all time, only 1,579 finish with an email.
  • Submit rate is already climbing: weekly 10.0% in late May to 12-13% in July.
  • The answers sell: "Tired of cheap tees" submits at 58.5%; Work wearers buy at 26%. Feed them to the email personalisation.

The moves, one line each

  • Alia: email and phone at stages 2 and 3, fewer questions, skips, offer visible with a code and expiry.
  • Email, Welcome: more emails, founder-led note instead of design, offer explicit.
  • Email, recovery: browse split prospect vs customer with the offer until they order; checkout gets a prospect version A/B tested hard; cart makes the offer unmissable.
  • Email, post purchase: first-time vs repeat flows, product care by product, founder note, add cross-sell and upsell.
  • Personalise everything by product: two categories, quiz data in hand, automations and campaigns.
  • WhatsApp: sort the Customer Created trigger, offer in every message, build browse and cart, add cross-sell, campaign to Kwikpass-recognised visitors about their waiting offer.
  • Win-back: later, once the above earns.
